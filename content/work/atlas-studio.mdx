## Overview

Atlas needed a marketing site that could carry a high-end design studio without feeling templated. We built it from the type system up.

## Approach

Started with a custom variable-weight typeface treatment, then designed the scroll spine first — every section inherits the same motion DNA so the whole site reads as one continuous performance.

## Outcome

Average session duration tripled. The site has been featured in two newsletters and one Awwwards SOTD.
