## Overview

Quill is a publishing platform that takes typography seriously. Every author gets a reading site that feels like it was hand-built for them.

## Approach

Built around restraint. Three layouts, four typefaces, generous whitespace. The reading experience is the whole product.

## Outcome

3,200 active publishers in the first year. Average read-through rate 2.3× the industry baseline.
