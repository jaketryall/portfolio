## Overview

Sundial reimagines the calendar as the home of your day, not a side panel. I joined as founding designer and built the visual language alongside the product strategy.

## Approach

Designed around the principle that time is a physical material — not a list. Built a flexible canvas that handles event blocks, focus blocks, and notes in one continuous spatial system.

## Outcome

Launched on iOS to 50k waitlist signups. Closed seed round 2 months after launch.
