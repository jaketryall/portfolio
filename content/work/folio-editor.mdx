## Overview

Folio is a writing tool for editorial teams. The challenge was building real-time collaboration without losing the focus of solo writing.

## Approach

Designed presence indicators that live at the edges of the workspace. Comments resolve into the margin. Suggestions thread inline. Everything secondary recedes when you're typing.

## Outcome

Shipped to 4,000+ paid teams. Featured in Product Hunt's editor-tool roundup.
