## Overview

North wanted to evolve from a fintech utility into a brand customers actually felt something about. We rebuilt the visual system from the ground up.

## Approach

Rebuilt the type and color systems first, then designed motion principles that carried into the product. Every transition uses the same easing family. The result is a product that feels considered rather than generic.

## Outcome

DAU up 24% in the quarter post-launch. App store rating climbed from 4.1 → 4.7.
