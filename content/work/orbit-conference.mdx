## Overview

Orbit needed an identity that could hold across web, print, signage, and a 60-foot stage backdrop. We built a generative system from the ground up.

## Approach

A single set of orbital primitives — rings, tangents, satellites — that compose into thousands of unique compositions. Each speaker got a generated poster. The web and signage shared the same visual seed.

## Outcome

Sold out in 11 days. Logo system has been adopted as the conference's permanent identity.
